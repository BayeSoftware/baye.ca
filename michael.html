Michael Traynor - Founder

Michael Traynor is the Founder and Developer at Baye Labs Inc. He has many years of experience working in software development and loves building tools that make complicated information easy for people to understand and use.

Michael has a background in Artificial Intelligence and works with both backend and frontend technologies. He believes technology can help people access information and make better decisions. His work at Baye Labs focuses on creating simple and helpful data-driven tools for everyone.
