Polity

Polity is our flagship product: an AI-native tool that makes legislation easier to read, track, and understand. It represents laws as a connected graph, so the complex web of cross-references and dependencies in legal text becomes something you can actually navigate.

  • See exactly what a bill changes, with amendments shown in the context of the acts they touch.
  • Compare versions of a bill side by side, from introduction through to law.
  • Understand definitions, cross-references, and dependencies without rebuilding them by hand.

It is built for the people who work closely with legislation — policy staff, legislative and parliamentary staff, law clerks, and government-relations and advocacy professionals.

Mission

At Baye Labs Inc., our mission is to make complex information more accessible and engaging for anyone looking to better understand data-driven topics. Inspired by Bayesian principles, we focus on providing clear, evidence-based insights while always linking back to original sources for transparency. Our goal is to empower users with tools that simplify data exploration and even make it fun to explore. We believe in turning learning into a smooth and enjoyable experience, helping everyone navigate complex subjects—like politics—with ease.

Previous Projects

VoteInformer was our first product — a civic-engagement app that helps people see which parties and politicians align with the policies they actually care about, moving the focus from political theatre toward real policy. It laid the groundwork for the legislative knowledge that powers Polity today.
